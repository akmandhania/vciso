Generic Cybersecurity Incident Response Plan (Comprehensive Template)
This template serves as a foundational document for an organization's overall incident response strategy.

I. Introduction & Purpose

Purpose: To define procedures and responsibilities for responding to cybersecurity incidents to minimize impact, restore operations, and protect critical assets (data, systems, reputation).

Scope: Covers all IT systems, networks, data, and personnel within the organization. Specifies which types of incidents are covered (e.g., malware, data breaches, DoS attacks).

Goals:

Protect confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.

Enable rapid recovery of business processes.

Ensure consistent response to threats.

Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
